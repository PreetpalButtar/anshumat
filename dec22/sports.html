FIT INDIA Movement was launched on 29th August, 2019 by Honourable Prime Minister with a view to make fitness an integral part of our daily lives. The mission of the Movement is to bring about behavioural changes and move towards a more physically active lifestyle. Towards achieving this mission, Annual sports meet-2022 of the Institute was held on 2ndDecember, 2022 in which around 650 boys and girls participated in various athletic events. The meet was inaugurated by Professor Shailendra Jain Director SLIET in the presence of faculty, staff and students. Students from all hostels participated in March past event along with NCC and NSS cadets. Total number of 84 medals were presented to students. Besides Director SLIET, Prof. Rajesh Kumar, Dean SW and Prof. A.S. Arora Dean FSW presided over prize distribution and closing ceremony.

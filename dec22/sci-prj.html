Sant Longowal Institute of Engineering and Technology Longowal organized 2nd state level science project contest for school students on 14th October, 2022 in the J.C. Bose Hall of the Institute. More than 600 students along with their teachers from100 schools of Punjab participated in the event. About 175 working models and 40 non-working models at various arena of focal theme “Integrated Approach in Science and Technology for smart agriculture with green environment” were displayed by students. Top three participants of Working Model Category were given Cash prize of Rs. 15,000/-; Rs. 10,000/- and Rs. 5,000/-, respectively, whereas, in Non-Working Model Category, Cash prize of Rs. 5000/-; Rs. 3000/- and Rs. 2000/- were given to 1st, 2nd and 3rd models, respectively, in addition to mementos and citations.

Prof. Shailendra Jain, Director of the Institute was the chief guest of the inaugural function. Prof. J.S. Dhillon Dean (Academics) graced the occasion as Guest of Honour. Prof. Rajesh Kumar Dean (SW) presided over the event. The whole event was coordinated by Prof. M. M. Sinha Convenor of the Science Project Contest. The very aim of the science project contest was to promote scientific temper among the school students in the state and also to fulfil the social responsibility of institute under Unnat Bharat Abhiyan, a flag ship campaign of Indian government. This will further generate the interest among the students to peruse science and engineering courses for their future career. The Chief guest and The Guest of Honour lauded efforts of the organizers for inculcating aspiration towards knowledge by harnessing their talent for innovation and creation. Director Prof. Shailendra Jain hoped that such contests will benefit the students of the state and would be helpful in their ability to recognise their potential and harness the same for career building.

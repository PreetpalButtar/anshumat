Discrimination Against Women Pakhwada from 25th November to 10th December, 2022

OTHER ACTIVITIES OF INTERNAL COMPLAINTS COMMITTEE (ICC) IN ASSOCIATION WITH CHIEF WARDEN GIRLS’ HOSTELS AND SPORTS DEPARTMENT

  • A Counselling Session on “Women Empowerment” was organized for Girls Students on 1st December, 2022 at Girls’ Hostel No. 3. Ms. Sharon Rattan, Psychological Counsellor, took the Session and delivered a talk for girls students. Around 100 students were present during the lecture.

  • Poster making Competition, Essay Writing Competition in English, Hindi and Punjabi and Poem Composition Competition on the broad areas of "Women empowerment, Gender Equality and Discrimination Against Women Means Injustice to the Entire Society" were organized.

  • “March for creating awareness” among staff and students regarding prevention of violence and discrimination against women in the society was also organized by the ICC in association with Sports Department of the Institute on 8th March, 2022. Dr. A.S. Arora, Dean (SFW) and the officiating Director, Dr. Rajesh Kumar, Dean (SW) and Dr. JapPreet Kaur Bhangu, Presiding Officer, ICC along with other faculty and staff members were present during the March. Around 150 students took part in the March.

The above activities were successfully organized by the ICC, which consists, Dr. JapPreet Kaur Bhangu, Presiding Officer, ICC, Dr. Parveen Kaur Khanna, Prof (M&H), Dr. Payal Malik, Member, ICC, Mr. Surinder Thakur, Member, ICC and Mr. Avtar Singh, Member, ICC.

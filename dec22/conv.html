Sant Longowal Institute of Engineering and Technology, Longowal celebrated its 25th Convocation on 20thDecember, 2022 which is also 34th foundation day of the institute. Prof. D.S. Chauhan, Former Vice Chancellor, Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow graced the occasion as Chief Guest and Prof. Rajeev Ahuja, Director, IIT, Ropar presided over the function. On this occasion, 16 Ph.Ds., 83 Post-Graduate, and 552 B.E. Degrees were conferred followed by a scintillating 14 Gold medals, 12 Silver medals and 12 merit certificate. At the onset, Prof. J. S. Dhillon, Dean Academics welcomed all the guests present on the occasion.

Prof. Shailendra Jain, Director SLIET congratulated the graduating students of 2022 who undertook their degrees under unprecedented circumstances due to Covid-19. In addition Director saluted and congratulated parents, guardians and benefactors for guiding and supporting students for their quest in achieving academic qualifications. He presented the Institute Annual Report 2022 which reflected the rapid strides made by the Institute despite the testing times of Covid 19. He reiterated that with the dedicated with the committed efforts of Team SLIET, the institute has made its presence felt in the technical arena both the national and international level.

Prof. D.S. Chauhan, in his Convocation Address congratulated the recipients of degrees and medals. He urged the recipients, that you have the required qualification, exposure the training to prove your mettle. Opportunities are many and the avenues are open for you to explore. All you need to do is to take the good of the past and relate it to the good of the present and make the future great. The ceremony concluded with a vote of thanks proposed by Registrar followed by the National Anthem.

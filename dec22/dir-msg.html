MESSAGE FROM DIRECTOR

“Execution is the key to success”

“Education without execution is extinction”

Newsletter of an educational institute is the mark and indication of its activities, mobility, achievements and activeness of a vibrant campus, necessary for the holistic development of students.

I am happy that institute is releasing 20th issue (Vol V, Issue IV) of ANSHUMAT, quarterly newsletter of SLIET. The successful completion of 5 years, is an indication of effective execution of the plan, key of success and effective education. I wish to congratulate the Team ANSHUMAT on this achievement, and hope that the institute will keep publishing the activities and achievements with the same spirit.

In the last five years, institute has achieved many milestones including NBA Accreditation of all seven UG programs, an indication to impart quality education. SLIET bagged “Outstanding Institution Award (Engineering College Category)” in the “Northern Region” in 2021, followed by “IEI-Engineering Education Excellence Award 2022” with “Certificate of Appreciation” in Dec 2022.

This shows the commitment and potential of its faculty, staff and students to achieve excellence. In the wake of NEP-2020, we look forward to fulfil our dreams to become a “Multi-Disciplinary University” through our vision plan “Vision SLIET 2030”.

I believe, with its unique mandate of multi-entry, multi-exit, modular pattern of education this institute will achieve greater heights in near future, as we SLIETians firmly believe in-

An Awareness Progamme on “Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013” for the employees and students of the Institute on 6thDecember, 2022. Dr. Shruti Goyal, Associate Professor, Department of Law, Rajiv Gandhi National University of Law, Patiala delivered a very informative lecture using Power Point Presentation to explain the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013. Around 200 participants attended the Awareness Program which included faculty, staff, outsourcing staff and Ph.D., PG and UG students.

HOSTEL NIGHT AT GIRLS HOSTEL NO. 01

Hostel night was organized in Girls Hostel No. 01 on 05.12.2022. All the students enthusiastically participated in the Cultural Eve. The main theme of the Hostel Night was “PARWAAZ’ to farewell to the seniors. The juniors paid gratitude to their seniors and seniors conveyed their best wishes to their juniors.

The hostel was beautifully decorated and the event was inaugurated by lamp lightening ceremony by Prof. Shailendra Jain, Director, SLIET and Mrs. Shobhana Jain accompanied by Dr. Rajesh Kumar (Dean (SW)), Dr. Pratibha Tyagi (Chief Warden (GH)), Dr. Sukhcharan Singh, Dr. Indraj Singh (Chief Wardens of BH), Dr. Prabhdeep Kaur and Dr. Payal Malik (Wardens of GH-01). The Evening was enlightened with beautiful cultural performances.

These cultural performances were followed by prize distribution ceremony, speech by Dr. Pratibha Tyagi, concluded with the vote of thanks to the Chair, students, as well as staff (GH-01) by Dr. Prabhdeep Kaur.

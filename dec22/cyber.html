EXPERT LECTURE ON “AWARENESS AND CAREER IN CYBER SECURITY” ORGANIZED BY HACKTEKERS SOCIETY

HackTekers Society has been established in the Institute with the collective efforts of students of ECE and CSE departments under coordination of Dr. Anupma Marwaha, Prof. (ECE) as Faculty Advisor with the main objective to develop a community of cyber security enthusiasts offering better Skillset and Career Opportunity.

An Expert Lecture on “Awareness and Career in Cyber Security” was conducted by the HackTekers Society on 01.12.2022 in the Mini Auditorium, CSE Block of the Institute. Mr. Mohit Yadav, CEO at Craw Security and Cyber Security Consultant, and his team members addressed the students and motivated them to grow and learn about cyber security and explore careers. More than 120 students participated in the event. The resource persons were felicitated by Er. Alka Singla and Er. Kuldeep Singh, Assistant Professors (ECE).

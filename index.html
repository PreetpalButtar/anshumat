An educational institution's newsletter serves as a symbol of the course of its activities, developments, and the dynamism of a campus that is essential for its advancement in general. Anshumat is the quarterly newsletter of SLIET. It was conceptualised by Prof. Shailendra Jain, honorable director of SLIET. The first issue of Anshumat was published in April 2017. Anshumat has successfully completed its 5 years with the release of its latest Jan-Mar 2023 issue.

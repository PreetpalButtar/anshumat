A chess tournament was held between Boys’ Hostel No. 7 and Boys’ Hostel No. 8. The tournament was played to improve the relationship between students and to motivate them to participate in different cultural activities. The event was coordinated by Dr. Vivek Kumar (Warden BH-07), Er. Divesh Bharti (Warden BH-07), Er. Sumit Kumar (Warden BH-08), Er. Rahul Gautam (Warden BH-08), Sh. Narender Garg (Caretaker BH-07 & BH-08) and members of sports committees of BH-07 and BH-08.

On 10thOctober, 2022, the first round was played between 16 players out of which 8 qualified for quarter-finals. On the same day, quarter-finals were held. On 11th and12th October, 2022, the semi-finals were held between the winners of the quarter-finalists. The finals took place on the same day between Abhishek (BH-07) and Abhiresh Ray (BH-08). The winner of the chess tournament was Abhiresh Ray of Boys’ Hostel No. 8.

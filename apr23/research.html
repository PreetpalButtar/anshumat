Research and Research Scholars Achievements

SLIET RESEARCH PROJECT AWARD (SRPA) AND SLIET QUALITY PUBLICATION AWARD (SQPA) ON REPUBLIC DAY 26TH JANUARY 2023

In order to promote Research environment in SLIET, Cash incentives and certificates were awarded to the faculty for obtaining externally funded Projects and to the research scholars for the publications in addition to the mandatory requirement of two research papers in the Web of Science journals with high impact factor.
